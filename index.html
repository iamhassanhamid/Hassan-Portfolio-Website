ABOUT ME


Hi there! I'm Hassan Hamid, a passionate graphic designer with a love for transforming ideas into visually compelling stories. With more than 4 years of experience in the industry, I bring a unique blend of creativity, technical expertise, and a commitment to delivering exceptional design solutions.

What sets me apart? It's the seamless fusion of artistic expression and strategic thinking. My designs not only captivate visually but also align with the core objectives of each project.

I specialize in:
1. Creating logos, business cards, and other elements that contribute to a company's visual identity.
2. Working with photography, including image selection, manipulation, and retouching, where I've honed my skills to create memorable and impactful visual experiences.
3. Manipulatng video and audio clips to create a seamless and visually appealing final product, and add visual effects, transitions, and other enhancements to improve the overall quality and style of the video.

From collaborating with ERS and Statement Jewels to spearheading projects that achieved 100% increase in engagement, I thrive on turning challenges into success stories. My design philosophy revolves around a meticulous blend of creativity and strategy. I believe in understanding the essence of a brand or concept, translating it into visuals that not only look good but also communicate effectively. Each pixel, color, and element serves a purpose in telling a story. Proficient in industry-standard tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After-Effects, Premiere Pro), I leverage the latest design trends and technologies to stay ahead.
My journey in graphic design began when i found myself building an interest in various company logos, for how simple and impacting they are. Since then, I've had the privilege of collaborating with a myriad of clients, each contributing to my growth and shaping my approach to design. Every project is a new opportunity to innovate and push creative boundaries.
